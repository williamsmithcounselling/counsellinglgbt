For Professionals: Supervision, Training & Resources

As fellow practitioners, our commitment to providing a safe, affirming space is paramount. Yet, working effectively with Gender, Sexuality, and Relationship Diversity (GSRD) requires more than good intentions; it demands broad understanding and specialist knowledge.

My own professional journey has led me to bridge the gap between deep academic theory and practical, in-the-room clinical application.

My MSc in Gender Studies provides a robust framework for understanding the systemic and cultural forces impacting our clients, while my BACP-accredited clinical practice supported by in-depth GSRD training keeps that knowledge grounded in the reality of therapeutic work. This is a space where you can bring your clinical questions, your 'stuck' points, and even your fears without judgment.

My goal is to provide the specialist support that empowers you to move beyond basic competency and into a practice of deep, confident, and sustainable affirming care.

Clinical Supervision

My supervision work focuses on building your confidence and navigating complexity within your practice. We'll explore your clinical work, your business, and you as the practitioner, with specialist support in Gender, Sexuality, and Relationship Diversity (GSRD). My goal is to help you cultivate a practice that is not just effective, but also deeply fulfilling and sustainable for the long term.
