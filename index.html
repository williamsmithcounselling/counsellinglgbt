About Your Therapist

Hello, I’m William (Will - he/him). I’m a BACP-accredited counsellor and supervisor dedicated to supporting the LGBTQ+ community. I believe that good therapy is built on genuine connection - a partnership where you feel seen, heard, and valued. My role is to help you make sense of your experiences without judgment.

My professional background is uniquely tailored to support this community. An MSc in Gender Studies provides me with a deep academic understanding of the social and cultural contexts that shape our lives. This is combined with clinical training in trauma-informed care and EMDR, allowing me to support clients with both insight and practical tools. My goal is simple: to bring my full, authentic self to our sessions, so you feel safe enough to connect with yours.
