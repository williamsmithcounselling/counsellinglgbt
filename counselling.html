My Commitment to You

An Understanding You Won't Have to Explain

You won't have to spend your sessions educating your therapist on LGBTQ+ identities or experiences. My academic background in Gender Studies, professional training in Gender, Sex and Relationship Diversity (GSRD) therapy, and my commitment to the community mean we can start our work from a place of shared understanding, allowing you to focus on what truly matters: you.

Collaborative & Effective Therapy

My role is to be your partner in this process. We will use proven, trauma-informed methods, but never in a rigid, one-size-fits-all way. I try to ensure sessions are tailored to your specific goals, whether that's building resilience, gaining insight, or navigating a specific life challenge.

A No-Pressure First Step

The free introductory chat is an opportunity for you to ask questions, get a feel for my style, and decide if I am the right fit for you. There is absolutely no pressure or obligation to continue. Finding the right therapist is the most important thing.
